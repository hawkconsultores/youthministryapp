Youth Ministry App

Empowering Adventist Youth Across the Inter-American Division

Discover the App

A Digital Ecosystem for Youth Ministry

Our mobile application serves as a comprehensive digital platform designed specifically for the Youth Ministry of the Inter-American Division. It creates a unified space where young Adventists can connect with their local clubs, participate in challenges, stay informed about ministry activities, and grow spiritually through structured programs and resources.

Connect

Build meaningful relationships within your local youth community

Engage

Participate in challenges and contests that strengthen faith

Grow

Access resources and programs for spiritual development

Key Features

Stage 1 – Ready by July 2025
User Registration, Profile & History

Register, create your profile, affiliate with a club, and track your progress and history.

Club Directory & Search

Browse and find registered clubs, view locations, and connect easily.

News & Official Announcements

Get the latest news and official communications, with push notifications and searchable history.

Youth Challenges & Contests

Participate in creative and missionary challenges, upload videos or testimonies, and see featured results.

Institutional Sponsors

View official sponsors through banners and mentions, strengthening support and institutional presence.

Stage 2 – After August 2025
Bible Connection Program & Event Materials

Daily access to the Bible Connection Program, spiritual content, and special event materials.

Event Calendar & Online Registrations

Interactive calendar with event details and online registration, including reminders and notifications.

Evangelism & Invitation Tools

Share Bible courses and invite friends to seminars, strengthening youth discipleship and outreach.

Administrative Web Platform

For Leaders and Administrators: The Administrative Web Platform is the backbone of the digital ecosystem, empowering church leaders at all levels (club, district, association, union, division) to manage, validate, and oversee all youth ministry operations.

This platform ensures structured governance, institutional alignment, and seamless integration with the mobile app—every feature used by youth is powered and validated by leaders through this administrative backbone.

User & Profile Management

Validate and approve member registrations, manage club changes, and maintain personal records.

Club & Director Management

Register, update, and manage clubs and directors, including status and activity.

News & Announcements Publishing

Publish and schedule news, announcements, and official communications for the mobile app.

Challenges & Contests Management

Create, monitor, and evaluate youth challenges and contests, including participant review and winner publication.

Sponsor & Banner Management

Register and manage sponsors, banners, and their visibility within the mobile app.

Bible Content & Event Materials

Upload and manage Bible Connection content, special event materials, and event registrations.

Evangelism Tools & Courses

Oversee and report on evangelism tools, courses, and seminars to support the church’s mission.
